How to Play

Test your knowledge of Pokémon card values in this fun and addictive guessing game.

Objective: The goal of the game is to guess the correct price of a randomly selected Pokémon card

Winning: If you correctly guess the price of the Pokémon card within the allowed number of guesses, you win the game! Celebrate your victory and consider challenging yourself with a new card for the next round.

Keep Playing: Challenge yourself to guess the prices of different Pokémon cards and improve your guessing skills. Each card presents a new puzzle to solve and an opportunity to test your knowledge of Pokémon card values.
